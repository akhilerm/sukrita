STEPS is a non-profitable student organization of TKM COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING which was established in 2011 with a vision of engendering social responsibility and compassion to the fellow beings, in the generations to come. STEPS approaches engineering with the motto "where engineering meets humanity.‚Äù

Over the years STEPS has conducted a plethora of activities both inside and outside the campus such as Stem cell registry camps, anti-drug campaigns, renovation of hospital equipments, educational activities which include free tuitions, scholarships and distribution of school kits, eye care facilities, blood donation camps, promotion of afforestation, visits to old age homes, awareness programs focused on atrocities against women & children, sustainable use of resources, promotion of safety and training as to how to deal with common accidents and hazards & integrity to the nation.

TKM College of engineering was found by Janab A. Thangal Kunju Musaliar in the year 1956 with a vision of excellence in education and research with socio-economic and environmental outlook. It has been the mission of the college, to offer state-of-the-art Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Doctoral programmes, to enhance knowledge by engaging in cutting edge research and by undertaking collaborative projects with industry, to instill ethical, social and environmental perspectives in designing systems for sustainable development and to nurture creativity, self learning and interpersonal skills.

As envisaged by the founder, T K M College of Engineering is an outstanding centre of excellence in Kerala, catering to the needs of higher education to all sections of society and stands an example of a model learning centre.
